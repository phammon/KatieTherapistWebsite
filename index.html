Services Overview

Children, Teens & Young Adults

Individual Therapy for Youth & Young Adults

I provide individual therapy for children, teens, and young adults who struggle with emotional regulation, anxiety, intense feelings, or behavioral challenges. My work is DBT-informed, meaning I focus on building practical skills while also creating a supportive, validating space.

Perinatal & Postpartum Mental Health

Therapy for Pregnancy & Postpartum (PMH-C)

I offer specialized support for pregnant and postpartum mothers navigating anxiety, mood changes, identity shifts, and the emotional realities of motherhood. As a Perinatal Mental Health-Certified therapist (PMH-C), I provide care that is informed, compassionate, and non-judgmental.
