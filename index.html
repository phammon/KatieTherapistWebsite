Therapy Services

Evidence-based approaches tailored to your unique needs

Individual Therapy

Traditional 50-minute therapy sessions where we'll explore your experiences, develop coping strategies, and work toward your goals in a supportive, non-judgmental environment.

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing is a powerful, evidence-based treatment for trauma, anxiety, and disturbing memories that helps your brain process and heal naturally.

EMDR Intensives

Extended 3-hour EMDR sessions designed for deeper processing and faster healing. Perfect for when you're ready to dive deep and make significant progress quickly.

Initial Consultation

A comprehensive 90-minute intake session where we'll discuss your background, current challenges, and therapy goals while determining if we're a good fit to work together.
